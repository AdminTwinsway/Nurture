About Us

Happiness is ‘seeing smile of gratitude from a patient’, and for a patient happiness is getting a quality care. This is achieved by the collective effort of all health care professionals in the system. And in this system role of a pharmaceutical company is inevitable. Being a responsible pharma company, we are committed to provide the best quality medicines, devices and services for a better health care ecosystem.

We believe “Spreading Happiness” is an endless effort to provide quality and affordability.

Nurture Health Care is an emerging pharmaceutical company specializing in drugs and devices. Our team is expertise more than fifteen years in pharmaceutical industry, the commitment and dedication of this team encourage us to do the best and more for our valuable consumers across India.

Mission and Vision

We at Nurture Health Care are committed to develop, manufacture and supply high quality, cost effective pharmaceutical products for the purpose of benefiting our valuable customers and improving their quality of life. We believe that in the long run Nurture Health Care will become a prominent global pharmaceutical company through our diligent efforts:

  • Continue to develop and expand our first class development, manufacturing and sales & marketing teams and create an effective, efficient, challenging and rewarding team working environment.
  • Create new product ideas and good business plans for developing drug delivery systems and new drug products to enhance our product portfolio through collaborating with our existing and new business partners for India and worldwide.
  • Be strictly compliant with WHO-GMP regulations and guidance and continuously improve our product manufacturing capabilities and quality assurance practices to ensure our pharmaceutical products are safe and effective
  • Leverage our strong sales & marketing leadership and capabilities to supply high quality and competitive products to the pharmaceutical marketplace in a timely manner.
  • Be environmentally responsible.
